Privacy Policy

Last updated 4 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Verisko Security ("Verisko", "we", "us") collects, uses and protects your personal information when you visit our website or request our services. By using this site or contacting us, you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

We only collect what we need to respond to you and provide our service:

  • Details you give us — when you submit our survey/contact form, this includes your name, phone number, optional email, the type of space you want to secure, and any message you send.
  • Information from contact — if you call, WhatsApp or email us, we keep the details needed to assist you and arrange a site survey.
  • Usage data — basic, non-identifying analytics such as pages visited and device type, used to improve the site.
  • Cookies — small files that help the site function and let us understand traffic. You can accept or decline non-essential cookies via the banner shown on your first visit.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry and arrange a free site survey.
  • To prepare quotes and recommend a suitable camera package.
  • To deliver, install and support the systems you purchase.
  • To improve our website and services.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for unrelated marketing without your consent.

Sharing your information

We share information only where necessary: with trusted service providers who help us run the business (for example, our website host) and where required by law. Our website is hosted on Vercel, which may process limited technical data (such as IP address) to serve the site securely.

Data retention

We keep enquiry and customer information only as long as needed to provide our services, honour warranties, and meet legal or accounting obligations — after which it is securely deleted.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information against loss, misuse and unauthorised access. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, but we work to keep your data safe.

Your rights

You may ask us to access, correct or delete the personal information we hold about you, or to stop contacting you. To make a request, reach us using the details below.

Children

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above shows when it was last revised. Significant changes will be reflected on this page.
