Questions

Things people ask us.

Quick, honest answers — before you book your free check.

Why does this cost more than the guy on the road?

Our cameras are real, not fake. Our cables are hidden, not nailed on the wall. We lock your system down so no one else can watch. And if anything breaks in the first year, we fix it free.

What happens when the power goes off?

Your video recorder has memory. Short power cuts don't lose footage. For longer ones, we can add a backup battery. We talk about it during your free check.

Can I add more cameras later?

Yes. Up to 2 more to any package. Or upgrade to a bigger one. Most of our customers start small and grow.

What if a camera stops working?

Call us. We come back free in the first year. After that, you can join our maintenance plan.

Do I own the cameras?

Yes. From day one. No subscription. No "give them back if you stop paying."

Can I see my cameras from anywhere?

Yes. We set up an app on your phone. You can watch live and look back from anywhere with internet.
