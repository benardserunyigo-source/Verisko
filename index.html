The Verisko standard.

Every Verisko install is built around six standards that don't get cut — regardless of package size or property type. Here's what comes with every job, from the first survey to the 30-day follow-up.

  • The right camera for each spot. 5MP bullet cameras for perimeter and gates. 5MP domes for interior coverage and identification-grade detail at counters and reception. 8MP wide-angle domes overhead at till points and other high-detail areas. 5MP PTZ for yards, drives, and parking. Every camera is matched to the work it has to do.
  • Concealed cabling. Every cable runs through proper trunking and conduit. Clean walls. A professional finish that matches a modern home or business interior.
  • Cybersecurity hardening built in. We change every default password, place your cameras on their own private network, enforce encrypted access only, and update firmware quarterly. Your footage stays yours — only the people you authorise can see what your cameras see.
  • Written warranty you can hold us to. Twelve months on hardware and installation, in writing, signed at handover. You always know where to find us.
  • Free 30-day follow-up. We come back at the 30-day mark to confirm the system still works the way it did on day one. No charge, no chasing.
  • One transparent price. Every cable, screw, connector, and configuration step is included in the price you see. No installation-day fees. No surprises.

The system you install today should still be protecting what matters to you next year. And the year after. That's why we build it carefully.
